Office & Parking
📍 Where do I park on Day 1?
▾
Parking is available at the basement level (B1–B2) of the Bangalore HQ building. Show your offer letter at the gate on Day 1 — your access card will be ready by Day 2. Bangalore-specific, not shown to Mumbai or remote hires.
💳 How does the health insurance benefit work?
▾
Health insurance coverage activates from Day 1 for Full-Time employees. Coverage details and the e-card download link are sent via email within the first week.
💻 My laptop isn't connecting to WiFi — what do I do?
▾
Connect to "Zfour-Guest" for the first hour. IT will configure your permanent network access by end of Day 1. If issues persist, raise a ticket from the IT Help section or contact the IT desk directly.
🗓 How does the Leave Policy work for new joiners?
▾
Leave accrues from your joining date on a pro-rata basis. You'll see your live balance in the Leave module from Day 1. Full policy document is linked here.
